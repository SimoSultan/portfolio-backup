A LITTLE EXTRA

In early 2018, I started building a web app for my business that allowed clients to book online. It was then that I found my absolute passion in life.


Originally a Civil/Structural Engineer, a Driver Trainer and a Business Owner. I am now pursuing my dream of becoming a programmer. At university, I still remember seeing a friend write a script that connected one program to another, I was bamboozled at how he did that! Still to this day I am amazed. But now, now I have the skills to make one myself. That feels like I have been living in a cardboard box for my entire life, and now I just stepped outside and can see the endless possibilities in the world.


THAT, is why I love being a programmer.
